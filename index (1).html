Welcome to Joelma Health Care

Joelma Health Care is accredited and regulated by the Care Quality Commission (CQC).

We spend time getting to know you and your way of life so we can provide relevant assistance and support. Our adult care services are tailored to your specific needs so you can choose the level of support you require — anything from a visit once or twice a week, several times a day through to 24-hour live-in care. No Joelma Health Care adult care services package is ever exactly the same because each person we support is different.

Joelma Health Care believes home care has the potential to transform people’s lives and their service supports older people develop real relationships, meet friends and maintain their presence in the community.

We provide care to people with a wide range of needs including:

*Older people, who may need assistance with personal care, medication and meal preparation, shopping and light cleaning.

People with health conditions, such as Dementia, Parkinson’s Disease or other complex needs.

Physical Disability.

Learning Disability.

Mental Health.

Respite Care.

Social Inclusion.

We help to match you with a fully trained carer who fits into your life and respects your space and dignity at all times. Every single carer is directly employed, expertly trained and managed by us. What’s more, we handle every aspect of your home care for you so you can focus on the important things and your independent.

We offer a wide range of services, tailored specifically for our service users Joelma Health Care provides a range of home care services that meet individual needs. These include but are not limited to domestic care, personal care, dementia care, community support, night care and mental health care. Joelma can provide 24-hours care to individuals living in their own home, and also offer personal assistance, meal preparation and support.

We pride ourselves on our quality of care and work closely with councils and CQC to ensure we're always on top

Joelma, strive to ensure that you get the best possible care, our staff are all DBS checked, and undertake quality assurance visits and quality control checks.
All our care workers will have either already attained or be in the process of attaining an QCF (former NVQ) in Health and Social Care.

If you would like to have a confidential discussion about our services, please contact us on 01582 343 477 / 07944 297048 or send us an email to Thelma.oba@joelma.co.uk
