About Us

Joelma Health Care provides all aspect of Care Services that involves matching and allocating Care Worker/ Workers to visit individual clients on a daily basis throughout the year.
Our Promise and commitment to supporting vulnerable adults and the elderly is determined and we do our utmost to keep you or your loved one living independently in the community.

If you need live-in care in Luton area or within the Bedfordshire region, please contact us today for a free information pack and a no obligation consultation with our Care Assessment Team on 01582 343 477.

  • Our live-in care is available 24 hours a day.
  • All live-in Clients will have access to a 24-hour live-in care on-call service covered by our dedicated team.
  • We place a great deal of importance on matching the right live-in carer with you.
  • All live-in care clients have access to a dedicated Live-in Care.

Please contact the office if you have any questions about our services or would like us to send you information about our home care fees.

All our care workers will have either already attained or be in the process of attaining an QCF (former NVQ) in Health and Social Care.

We are dedicated to serving our customer's needs, and our goal is 100% customer satisfaction. We strive to bring you the best in quality service. If something isn't right, please let us know.
